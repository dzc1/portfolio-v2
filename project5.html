Case Study Description

The second project at Ironhack consisted in creating a set of wellness tools for the National Wellness Institute with the objective of generating a new visual system that reflects their innovative and refreshed approach.


Trust me, benchmarking its not easy. Its fun but, certainly not easy. So after accepting a small defeat I retrieved to my house to relax and rest.


At home I came across this documentary talking about addiction, I started watching it (having a family member fall upon addiction myself), I felt related to some of the stories and the documentary touched diverse aspects of what constitutes addiction.


After finishing watching it, my head was clear and my decision was made.

User Research

“It is a capital mistake to theorize before one has data. Insensibly one begins to twist facts to suit theories, instead of theories to suit facts” - Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.

In order to design a informative, trustworthy and collaborative platform, I needed to get in deep detail on the main problems and frustrations current and former addicts were going through.


Due to my time limitations I divided the research into surveys created in Typeform and face to face interviews to get both qualitative and quantitative data. From this data, the major pain points I was able to point out were the following.


Current and former addicts have specific triggers that enables a relapse into their addictions. These trigger's can be avoided by having a companion that helps you by listening and providing guidance when such tirggers happen.


These individuals in the addiction world are called "Sponsors".
